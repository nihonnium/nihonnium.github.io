Production diary – Week 15

Date: 13 February 2026

This week I worked on my GitHub Pages portfolio website. I focused on creating or improving the main sections (About, Projects, Skills, Contact).

I am most happy with the clear organization of my projects section. Using the semantic <article> tags allowed me to distinctively highlight my UAV Patent Application and Research work. I also get some inspration from the other websites for the styling.

I found it difficult to decide on the best way to present my non-technical achievements alongside my coding skills. Since I have a lot of experience in leadership (like The 93% Club) and social innovation, balancing these with my CS skills required careful planning of the page layout. I also spent some time refining the navigation bar to ensure the hover effects worked smoothly.

From this work, I learned the importance of semantic HTML tags. Instead of using generic div tags for everything, I used <header>, <main>, and <section>, which helped me understand how to structure a webpage for better readability and potential accessibility.

Next time I want to improve my portfolio by adding the "CS Maths & Tools" page. Specifically, I plan to start working on the JavaScript required to build a simple interactive tool, such as a number base converter, to demonstrate my programming logic on the web.
